This is currently the hottest area in AI.


```text id="8h22w4"

   ↓
AI plans tasks
   ↓

   ↓
Executes workflows
   ↓
```

This is called:

- Agentic AI,
- autonomous AI systems,
- AI agents.

# 5. Small Language Models (SLMs)

This is a VERY important trend.

People realized:

- giant models are expensive,
- slow,
- difficult to deploy.

Now the industry is shifting toward:

- specialized smaller models.

This is called:

- SLMs (Small Language Models). ([Gleecus TechLabs Inc.][5])

## Why It Matters

SLMs are:

- cheaper,
- faster,
- deployable on edge devices,
- easier for enterprises.

Future architecture:

```text id="9n1jdi"
Large Model → Complex Reasoning
Small Models → Specialized Tasks
```

This hybrid architecture is becoming dominant.
